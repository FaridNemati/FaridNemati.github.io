About Me

Hi! My name is Farid. I am a Photographer - Videographer from Uruguay, based in Berlin since 2017. I am passionate about the art of still and moving images that tell inspiring stories in diverse ways.

Creating compelling content that meets people's expectations while promoting good values and representation is what I aim for. I also enjoy working on film sets  mostly as a camera assistant and gaffer.
